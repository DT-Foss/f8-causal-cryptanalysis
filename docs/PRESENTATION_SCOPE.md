# Nanjing presentation scope

The source presentation
`paper/nano2026/presentation/Foss_CASI_Nano-IoT_IEEE_NANO_2026.pptx` was used
to define the consolidation scope. Its 15 slides connect three layers:

1. `.causal` as the general hidden-structure lineage.
2. CASI as a black-box single-output structural/compression score.
3. F8 as the cross-round mechanism probe for ARX carry dependence.

The presentation reports 41 implementations across nine families, all 10 Speck
variants, the SIMON negative-control family, a fitted beta-dependent Speck law,
and a separate full-round Threefish-256 observation. It also reports robustness,
sample sensitivity, and four implementation-fault detections in SKINNY/KATAN.

This repository keeps the 41-cipher CASI registry, Nano-IoT data, paper TeX,
and author-owned PowerPoint. The public deck differs only in sanitized document
metadata; slide content and media are unchanged. Its publication hash and the
exact metadata transformation are recorded in `docs/PRIOR_ART.md`.
