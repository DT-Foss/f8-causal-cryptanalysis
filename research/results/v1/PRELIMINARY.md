# Preliminary follow-up results

The JSON files in this directory were generated before the historical paper
reproduction was closed. They are retained as research breadcrumbs, but no
finding here is promoted as a new result yet.

In particular, `casi_calibration.json` predates the Speck/SIMON KAT correction.
Cipher-dependent portions must be rerun under `corrected_v1`; null-calibration
portions may be reused only with an explicit source/version record.
